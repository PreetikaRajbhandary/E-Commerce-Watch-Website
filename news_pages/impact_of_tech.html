Impact of Technology in Watch Industry

Sept 7, 2024



The impact of technology has fully changed the whole watch industry. Technology has changed the watch industry in many ways. As before technology in watch industry people has no choice in watches they have only 2 options either classic analog or digital only showing time but now after technology as introduced. Now people have many options such as luxury watch , classic anolog watch, digital, hybrid, and many more.

As smart watch got introduced luxury brands are also including technology in their watches keeping the classic old loook but adding functionality in it resulting an hybrid watch. They were doing so to survive in the market.
Smart watches are really smart. They features like GPS, health tracking, notification, call, and many more which is becoming possible due to technology. Due to technological equipment and machineries the watches are being manufactured rapidly and accurately which results in fulfilling the demands in the market easily. As of now watch industry is focusing on environment friendly watches using solar energy to charge the watch. All of this is becoming possible due to the Technology.So, technology has great impact in watch industry.

Positive Impact of Technology

  • Faster and precised production of watches with effective cost due to implementation of technology.
  • It enables to add more features like health tracking and many more.
  • Due to technology now company can sell their product online using online e-commerce platform or by creating their own platform.


Negative Impact of Technology

  • Many people who used to make watch or work in manufacturing the watch now about (80-90)% they have been replaced by technology.
  • Manufacturing of traditional watches has been reduced because consumer/customer are intrested in smart watch.
  • As smart watches are connected to the internet the risk of user data and privacy increased.
  • Smart watch company collects the user data to know user preference and also sell those data to other company.
