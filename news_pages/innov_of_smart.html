Innovation of Smart Watch

July 1, 2024



The smart watch in 1970's in the smart watch industry was very simple having key features like alarm and calculators. Over the time they become more advanced and started to dominate the market.

As of now they have many smart features due to advancement in technology. Today's smart watch can do several things like

  • Tracking health (heart rate, blood oxygen, sleep)
  • Having fitness feature(steps, workouts, GPS)
  • Have voice assistant
  • Can make call, show notification, send message
